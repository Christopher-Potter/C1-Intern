How it works

make your selection

To obtain your restaurant recommendations, start by entering in a value for how much you care about various factors associated with dining. Preferences can take on values from -10 to 10, depending on if you want to maximize or minimize a particular factor. For example, if you want a fancy dining experience close to home, you might enter an 8 for cost to indicate a desire for more expensive restaurants, but a -7 for distance to indicate a preference to avoid travelling very far. Preference values of 0 and blank preference boxes indicate that you don't care at all about a particular factor. So, if you're ambivalent to the number of flamingos near the restaurant, but a 0 in the flamingos field, or leave it blank.

Once you've filled in all of your preferences, hit the submit button to see a list of the restaurants that best match up with your indicated preferences. Please note that to receive relevant results, you must permit Eat Easy to use your location.
